`Resazurin/data/20260624-mgig-33C`

## DESCRIPTION

Adult oysters from nine USDA families were placed in plastic cups and submerged in ~100mL of freshly prepared resazurin working solution at room temperature (~22<sup>o</sup>C). Cups were incubated in a water table at 33<sup>o</sup>C. At designated timepoints, 200uL of resazurin was sampled from each cup and placed in a 96-well plate. Flourescence measurements were taken with a Synergy HTX (Agilent) plate reader.
