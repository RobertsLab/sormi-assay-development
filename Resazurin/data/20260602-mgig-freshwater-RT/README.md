`Resazurin/data/20260602-mgig-freshwater-RT`

### Description

Juvenile oysters from nine USDA families were distributed across nine 12-well plates and submerged in 4mL of freshly prepared resazurin working solution made with *TAPWATER*. Plates were incubated at room temperature and fluorescence was measured on a Synergy HTX (Agilent) plate reader periodically to assess changes in oyster metabolism.


NOTE: Room temperature changed drastically on Day 0 of the experiment. The samples started at ~21<sup>o</sup>C, but by the final measurement, the temperature had increased to ~27<sup>o</sup>C.

---

## FILES

- `*-ImageJ.csv`: Area measurements calculated with ImageJ. A corresponding image of the plate can be viewed in the `images` directory.

- `layout.csv`: A metadata file containing plate layouts, sample IDs, oyster measurements, etc.

- `plate-[A-Z]-T[0-9].[0-9].txt`: Data files from the plate reader. The `T[0-9].[0-9]` represents the time, in hours, after the experiment began.
